About Eoghan

Eoghan Hand is an Irish Lighting Designer working in the Broadcasting, Film and Video sectors. Having completed his Broadcasting Skills Production Course in 2017, Eoghan progressed on to working as a Lighting Trainee on Season 3 of RTE's homegrown comedy Bridget and Eamon, before becoming a Lighting Director on several indie short films and music videos.

His passion for filmmaking is evident through his commitment to continued professional development, using techonologies he has acquired in his extensive Professional Lighting Kit to create the most appropritate lighting designs for film sets, videos and commercials.

Check out some of Eoghan's work below to learn more.

Repak Ireland Commerical

Four Titan Tubes were rigged to a ceiling overhead with two sheets of diffusion underneath for this set up. Black cloth drapped on all four sides to firect the lights only on the subjects. One or two leds were used in the background to raise the ambiance levels.

Rudimental | Straight From The Heart

The house setup was a 4K HMI coming through the window with straw. It was complimented by two Rayzr panels that were red and green. On the streets and in around the shopping center we used 14 Titan Tubes, rigged up high with 4 additional tubes used free hand.Two 600D's were used to increase ambiance levels gelled red at the shopping center.

Izzy's Wheels | Microsoft Surface

The street scenes were mostly handheld with 250 disffusion frame. The studio scene was a 2.5 HMI bounced into a 8x8 grid. A 1.2 HMI was spotted onto the background to create depth while two Rayzr Panels were used as side lights.
